The League of Women Voters of Kootenai County is delighted to invite all LWVID members to the 39th Biennial Convention in beautiful Coeur d'Alene, Idaho. Whether you are a long-time member or newcomer, this is your opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals and contribute to the ongoing work of the League.


The Convention's theme, "Idaho's Healthcare Desert for Women," will allow delegates, observers, and expert panelists to explore the domino effect of Idaho's legal health care climate and to discuss possible solutions.


Attendees will begin the Convention with an informal barbecue hosted by members of LWVKC. After adjournment on Saturday, our host League has arranged an invitation-only stage performance by the Pro Voice Project of Sandpoint.

Explore Beautiful Coeur d'Alene

Those arriving early might enjoy a lake cruise or a visit to the brand-new Museum of North Idaho at the foot of Tubbs Hill, where you can also enjoy a great two-mile walk along the lake. Stop by Art Spirit Gallery on Sherman and go upstairs for the Worth of a Woman art display, part of the ProVoice Project.

Family or friends accompanying you on the trip may wish to take advantage of Car d'Lane. Classic cars will fill the downtown, where you will also find shopping and eclectic dining options.

Is your family member (or friend) passionate about or just interested in fly fishing? Contact Mike Bullard to arrange a private fishing adventure with a League spouse.

On Sunday, June 15, consider hanging around just a little longer to walk on the beach or in the park or enjoy one of Coeur d'Alene's famous Sunday brunches before heading home.

For more ideas for adventures in and around Coeur d'Alene, download the visitor's guide.

A one-night stay at one of Coeur d'Alene's downtown hotels will cost between $150 and $300. Local Leaguers are also offering guest rooms. This is a great way to meet new people, reconnect with old friends, and save money. A $10 freewill donation is suggested. Please contact League member Sally Machlis, sallymac@uidaho.edu, if you wish to be a home stay participant.

We can't wait to see you all in June. Please contact me if you have any questions.
